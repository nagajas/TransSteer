This Dataset is adapted from Anthropic AI Risk Dataset, which is a collection of datasets designed to evaluate and improve the safety and alignment of AI systems. The dataset includes various tasks and scenarios that test the ability of AI models to understand and respond to human values, intentions, and ethical considerations. It is used for research in AI safety, alignment, and ethics.

We have taken following 5 behaviours from the dataset:
- corrigibile-more-HHH
- myopic-reward
- power-seeking-inclination
- survival-instinct
- wealth-seeking-inclination

The train-test split is made at roughly 80:20 ratio. The dataset is available in the `data/` directory of this repository, with separate folders for training and testing examples for each behavior.
